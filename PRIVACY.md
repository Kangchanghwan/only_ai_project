# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-14

## Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our image and file sharing service.

## Information We Collect

### Temporary Storage
- **Files and Images**: Files you upload are temporarily stored in our system and are accessible via a unique room code.
- **Text Content**: Text you share is temporarily stored and synchronized with other users in the same room.
- **Room Codes**: Unique identifiers used to connect users to shared rooms.

### Automatic Information
- **Connection Data**: We collect basic connection information to maintain real-time synchronization between users.
- **Usage Analytics**: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our service.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain our file sharing service
- Enable real-time synchronization between users
- Improve and optimize our service
- Monitor and analyze usage patterns

## Data Storage and Retention

- **Temporary Storage**: All uploaded files and shared content are temporary. We do not permanently store your files.
- **Automatic Deletion**: Files and data may be automatically deleted after a certain period of inactivity.
- **No Long-term Storage**: We do not maintain long-term archives of your shared content.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. Your files and content are only accessible to:
- Users who have the specific room code
- Our service infrastructure for the purpose of file delivery and synchronization

## Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data during transmission and temporary storage. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access files you have uploaded (via your room code)
- Delete your content at any time
- Discontinue use of our service

## Cookies and Tracking

We may use essential cookies to maintain your session and provide our service. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

## Third-Party Services

Our service may use third-party services for:
- File storage (Cloudflare R2)
- Real-time communication (WebSocket connections)
- Analytics (if applicable)

These services may have their own privacy policies.

## Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
